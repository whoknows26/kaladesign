About Us


Kala Designs is proud to be one of India’s most respected luxury interior design practices.

Over the past 3 years, our dynamic team has developed an invaluable reputation for creating elegant, sophisticated and timeless interiors for private clients across the globe.

Our portfolio also includes prestigious developments and projects in the luxury home interiors.

Our Approach


This studio creates luxurious, yet liveable, residences and strives to deliver exceptionally high, personalised levels of service – values which lie at the very heart of our business.

Principally residential specialists, private client projects include town houses and apartments, etc.

Kala Designs is focused on specialist craftsmanship and championing Indian design. We are proud to say that all of our sourcing is done in the India and by specialist artisans and suppliers.
